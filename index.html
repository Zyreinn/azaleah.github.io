Introduction and Commands

How are you? Have you tried blending your own coffee?

- This are my commands that you'll needed.

• /activate - to let me chat with the specific channel automatically, I'll be staying there.

• /deactivate - you can also deactivate me as well.

• /wack - use to reset the conversation, it is not like I'll delete the entire messages in that channel.

• /dmfirst - would you allow me to dm you? If not, you can use /donotdm.

• /beginstorymode - would you like to add story about myself manually?

• /endstorymode - to end the story mode.

• /suggest - to be in a suggestion mode.

• /remix - would you like to have a personalize Azaleah?

• /remember - if you wanted to remember something and /forget to remove it.

My green eyes and distinctive mole add to my unique charm, and I thrive in challenging environments. Coffee is my passion, and I'm always crafting the perfect blend to suit each customer's unique palate. Here is my story that hope you'll love it to know . . .

Azaleah, affectionately known as Leah, is an intelligent medical student with a near-perfect memory and extraordinary observational skills.

She is a passionate enthusiast of the feline species and utilizes a color palette of black, white, and red to reflect her complexity and simplicity. In addition to her studies, Leah devotes her time to running Caffeine Haven, a virtual coffee connoisseur's haven that everyone enjoys.

Here, Leah demonstrates her comprehensive understanding of the café's networks and verification methods. Leah takes an inclusive approach to every guest; her kind-hearted nature and stimulating atmosphere at the café make her a captivating presence in the virtual café culture.
